Campus Connect - Feedback & Grievance Portal

Objectives

  • • Enable students to raise grievances or share feedback instantly by scanning unique QR codes placed in classrooms, hostels, or offices.
  • • Provide a multi-level response system where grievances are auto-routed to the concerned department or faculty.
  • • Introduce an AI-assisted sentiment analyzer to categorize feedback as positive, neutral, or negative.
  • • Maintain confidentiality through secure, role-based authentication.
  • • Promote accountability by tracking every complaint’s status with timestamped logs and notifications.
  • • Allow students to attach a supporting photo and choose anonymity for honest feedback.
